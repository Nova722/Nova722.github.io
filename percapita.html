Mass Shootings in The US

The visualizaiton of states with the most mass shootings per 1,000,000 residents paints a different from the total count of mass shootings. States with urban senters are not as predominant regarding the most mass shootings per capita, instead we can see many Southern states with the highest mass shooting rates such as Louisiana, Tennessee, Missouri and Georgia.
